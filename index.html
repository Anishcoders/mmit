Mahamaya Polytechnic Of Information Technology, Shravasti

Mahamaya Polytechnic of information Technology, lengadi goolar Shravasti Was established in the year 2016. It is situated on Shravasti Gilaula,Bhinga road nearly 30 kms from district headquarters Bhinga Shravasti. Today it houses in its 5.0 acre campus, a main building, students hostels, library, classrooms for various faculties including Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Food Technology. It's aim is to become one of the best Polytechnic College in U.P. It has assiduously fostered a culture of collective responsibility to shape the destiny by empowering the students with employability skills of intelligence, discipline, etiquettes, self- confidence, humility and hard work.   View More
